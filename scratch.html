Volleyball Basics



Volleyball is played with very simple rules:
  1. Game Objective
    • Each team tries to ground the ball on the opponent’s court.
    • The first team to reach 25 points (with at least a 2-point lead) wins the set.
    • Matches are usually best of 5 sets.

  2. Serving the Ball
    • A rally starts with a serve from behind the baseline.
    • The ball must go over the net and land inside the opponent’s court.
    • Players must serve in rotation order.

  3. Hitting & Rotations
    • Each team has 3 hits to return the ball (bump, set, spike).
    • No player can hit the ball twice in a row.
    • Teams rotate clockwise after winning the serve back.

  4. Violations
    • Touching the net, double contacts, or carrying the ball are faults.
    • A ball landing out of bounds gives a point to the other team.
    • Stepping over the centerline is also a violation.
